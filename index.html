Adventure at St. John Island and Lazarus Beach!

St. John Island

Take a ferry from Marine South Pier Ferry Terminal for a fun filled adventure of one of Singapore's relaxing Island for on $15/person ($11 for child below 16) . It only takes about 15 minutes of ferry ride to get to the Island. Feel free to take a walk to explore peaceful island and get some fresh air while you are at it. Sit by the beautiful beach and have a picnic with your friends and family!

    Things to note!
  • Remember to bring loads of water as there is no water point on the Island.
  • Bring food and snacks if you are going to stay the whole day as there are not restaurants or shops.
  • There are only 2 toilets on the Island so do take note of their locations.

Lazarus Beach

Tired of just visiting sentosa beaches,East Coast beach and Changi Beach because of unclear water? Don't worry, you are in luck. St. John Island is now linked to Lazarus Island by a mini courseway bridge. Take a 10mins walk from the jetty at St.John Island across the courseway bridge to a secluded beach on Lazarus Island. It is a famous place that both local and tourist go to have their diving sessions due to the crystal clear water and beautiful coral bays. If you are in luck, you can see many beautiful yachts docked nearby as people visit the island and enjoy the clear water.

    Things to note!
  • Remember to bring loads of water and food also as there is also no water point or shops on this island.
  • Sadly, there is no toilet near the beach and hence, make sure to go at St.John Island before you start your adventure
  • There are shelthered areas for you to put your belongings as you enjoy yourselves.
